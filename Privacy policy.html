Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how the Discord Rich Presence Tool ("the Tool") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use it.

1. Information We Collect

The Tool stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Your Discord Application Client ID
  • Your Rich Presence configuration settings (such as details, state, image keys, and buttons)

No data is collected or transmitted to any servers or third parties by the Tool.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to update your Discord Rich Presence status via the Discord API.

3. Data Storage and Security

All your data is stored locally in a file named config.json on your device. The Tool does not send or share your data externally.

4. Third-Party Services

The Tool uses the Discord API to set Rich Presence statuses. Please review Discord's own privacy policies regarding their API usage.

5. Your Rights

You have full control over your data stored by the Tool. You can delete the config.json file at any time to remove your stored information.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Continued use of the Tool after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer.
